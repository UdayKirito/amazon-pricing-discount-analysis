# 📊 Amazon Pricing & Discount Strategy Analysis

## 📌 Problem Statement
Understanding how pricing and discount strategies impact product performance is critical for maximizing revenue while maintaining customer trust.

This project analyzes Amazon product data to identify key pricing drivers and evaluate how discounts influence customer ratings and engagement.

## 📁 Dataset
- Amazon product dataset  
- Features include: price, discount percentage, ratings, reviews, and product categories  

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Statistical Analysis (ANOVA, Regression)
- Jupyter Notebook

## 🔍 Analysis Performed
- Conducted ANOVA tests to analyze differences across pricing groups  
- Built regression models to identify key drivers of product performance  
- Evaluated relationship between discount levels and customer ratings  


## 📈 Key Insights
- Moderate discounts resulted in higher customer satisfaction compared to extreme discounts  
- Price sensitivity varied significantly across product categories  
- Certain price ranges consistently outperformed others in ratings and engagement  


## 💡 Recommendations
- Avoid excessive discounting to maintain brand perception  
- Implement category-specific pricing strategies  
- Focus on optimal pricing bands instead of aggressive promotions  


## 🚀 Business Impact
This analysis helps businesses balance revenue growth with customer trust, enabling more sustainable pricing strategies.
